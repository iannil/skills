**EN**: ""Observed consensus"" is a possible snapshot of ""consensus"" across multi-level consciousness, enabling coherent subjects to share a broadly aligned reality while minor observational divergences are ignored. Within the snapshot, stable and continuously reinforced paths of possibility-convergence may form—that is, the law of karma. From the human perspective, the law of karma appears a priori and can be expressed through logic, mathematics, physics, and other forms. Those incoherent subjects outside the snapshot either recede into the base of possibility or constitute the reality of the other. So-called high-dimensional or low-dimensional does not refer to physical space, but to different constructions of reality converged from the base of possibility through distinct modes of observation.
